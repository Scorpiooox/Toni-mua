About Me

Antonia has over five years’ experience working as an editorial make-up artist for the likes of the TOWIE stars, catwalk fashion models and music artists.

After studying at London College of Fashion where she learned the art of Runway, Haute-Couture and Fashion Editorial make-up, Antonia went onto work with the legendary makeup artist Louise Constad, assisting her with makeup courses to many of her clients.
